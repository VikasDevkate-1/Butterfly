All About Butterflies

Butterflies belong to the order Lepidoptera, which includes both butterflies and moths. They are known for their remarkable life cycle and beautiful wings.

  • Life Cycle: Egg, Larva (Caterpillar), Pupa (Chrysalis), Adult (Butterfly)
  • Diet: Nectar from flowers, rotting fruit, tree sap
  • Habitat: Meadows, forests, gardens, and wetlands
